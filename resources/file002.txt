article 2: the role of artificial intelligence in modern programming. artificial intelligence (ai) plays a crucial role in modern programming by providing tools for solving complex tasks, automating processes, and enhancing user experience. ai systems learn from large datasets, enabling them to predict user behavior, analyze data, and offer optimal solutions. in programming, ai is used to create chatbots, recommendation systems, automated testing, and improve search algorithms. integrating ai into software development allows for the creation of smarter and more adaptive applications that can meet the ever-changing needs of users.
